Analysis of Overwatch 2 Season 2 Competitive Data

 Posted on April 17, 2022  |  1 minutes  |  159 words  |  Mujtaba Bader

Project Overview

This is a project I’m currently working on, as of now it’s not my primary focus but it’s something I’m doing on the side. Using season 2 competitive data I’d like to try and come to some conclusion on the balance state of the game during season 2. As of now I’ve completed most of the analysis, I’ve looked at the data for Tank win-rate and pick-rate, I’ve also done the same for supports. Though I feel the data I’ve got doesn’t tell the full story of the balance state of the game, the subjective experience of the players depending on their skill tier is something that should be taken into account when coming to any conclusions, as such it’s quite difficult to generalize given the data.
